---
title: Grandma's Rock Buns
servings: 9 Buns
prep: 15 Min
cook: 15 Min
source: Handed down from Grandma in Nelson, England
---

## Ingredients

- 8 oz plain flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp mixed spice
- 4 oz margarine
- 4 oz caster sugar
- 6 oz mixed fruit
- 2 oz desiccated coconut
- 2 eggs, beaten

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, baking powder, and mixed spice into a bowl.
2. Chop the margarine and rub it into the flour mixture until it reaches a breadcrumb consistency.
3. Add the sugar, coconut, and mixed fruit and mix well.
4. Stir in the eggs.
5. If needed, add a very small amount of water until the dough reaches a soft dropping consistency — it drops off the spoon in about 5 seconds.
6. Scoop into about 9 buns on a baking sheet. Bake at gas mark 7 (220°C / 425°F) for 15 minutes.
