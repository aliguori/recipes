---
title: Fruit Rock Cakes
servings: 12 Rock Cakes
prep: 1½ Hrs
cook: 20 Min
source: Adapted from Food Wishes' Strawberry Rhubarb Drop Biscuits
---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup each diced rhubarb and strawberries
- 2 tbsp white sugar
- 2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- ⅓ cup brown sugar
- 1½ tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 3 tsp ground cinnamon
- ½ tsp ground nutmeg
- ½ tsp ground allspice
- ¼ tsp ground cloves
- ¼ tsp ground ginger
- 6 tbsp butter, cold and sliced
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Toss the rhubarb, strawberries, and white sugar in a bowl. Cover and macerate in the fridge 1–2 hours.
2. Preheat oven to 400°F and line a baking sheet with parchment.
3. Whisk together the flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and spices.
4. In a small bowl, whisk the egg, vanilla, and ¼ cup of the milk.
5. Drain the fruit; add 2 tbsp of its syrup to the egg mixture and pat the fruit dry.
6. Rub the butter into the flour mixture to a breadcrumb consistency, then mix in the fruit until coated.
7. Stir in the egg mixture, then add the remaining milk a little at a time until it just comes together — drier and shaggier than plain rock cakes.
8. Scoop into 12 craggy balls on the baking sheet.
9. Bake at 400°F for 15 to 20 minutes, until golden at the top and edges. Cool on a wire rack.
