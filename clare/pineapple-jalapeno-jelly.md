---
title: Spicy Pineapple Jelly
servings: 8 Half-Pints
prep: 20 Min
cook: 15 Min
source: Adapted from Taste of Home's Cranberry Jalapeño Jelly
---

## Ingredients

### Spicy Pineapple Juice
- 46 oz can pure pineapple juice
- 2 jalapeño peppers, sliced

### Jelly
- 3½ cups spicy pineapple juice
- ½ cup white vinegar
- 7 cups sugar
- 2 (3 oz) pouches liquid pectin

## Directions

1. **Make the juice:** Strain the pineapple juice through 4 layers of cheesecloth.
2. Add the sliced jalapeños (with seeds), let sit 2 minutes, then strain through a sieve. Longer steeping gives a spicier jelly.
3. Filter through 1 coffee filter, then measure out 3½ cups.
4. **Make the jelly:** Combine the juice and vinegar in a pot and heat over medium until warm.
5. Add the sugar and heat, stirring constantly, until fully dissolved.
6. Raise heat to medium-high and bring to a full rolling boil, stirring constantly.
7. Stir in the pectin, return to a full rolling boil, and boil 1 minute. Remove from heat.
8. Skim off foam and ladle into hot jars, leaving ¼″ headspace. Process in a boiling water bath 10 minutes.
