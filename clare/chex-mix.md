---
title: Chex Mix
servings: 13–14 Cups
prep: 10 Min
cook: 6 Min
source: Adapted from Culinary Hill's Chex Mix
---

## Ingredients

### Mix
- 3 cups Corn Chex cereal
- 3 cups Rice Chex cereal
- 3 cups Wheat Chex cereal
- 2 cups Cheez-Its crackers
- 2 cups snaps pretzels

### Seasoning
- 8 tbsp (1 stick) unsalted butter
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tsp Lawry's seasoned salt
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp onion powder

## Directions

1. In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine the Chex cereal, Cheez-Its, and pretzels.
2. In a small bowl, microwave the butter until melted, then stir in the Worcestershire sauce, seasoned salt, garlic powder, and onion powder.
3. Pour the butter mixture over the cereal in three batches, stirring well after each.
4. Microwave on high 6 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes.
5. Spread on paper towels to cool. Store in airtight containers at room temperature up to 5 days.
