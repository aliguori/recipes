---
title: Butternut Squash Sauce
servings: 6 Servings
prep: 5 Min
cook: 5 Min
---

## Ingredients

- 1 (15oz) can butternut squash puree
- ½ cup pasta water
- ½ cup sofrito
- 2 tbsp olive oil

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients in a small pot and bring to a simmer.
2. Store in the fridge for up to a week.
3. Also works with canned pumpkin or sweet potato puree — adjust the olive oil and pasta water to reach a sauce consistency.
