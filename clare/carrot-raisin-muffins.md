---
title: Carrot Raisin Muffins
servings: 12 Muffins
prep: 30 Min
cook: 27 Min
source: Adapted from Brooklyn Active Mama's Carrot Muffins
---

## Ingredients

- 1½ cups white whole wheat flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt
- 3 tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp nutmeg
- 1 cup applesauce
- 1 egg
- ¼ cup honey or maple syrup
- 4 tbsp melted butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup grated carrots
- ⅓ cup golden raisins

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F and spray a muffin pan with cooking spray.
2. In a small bowl, mix the carrots and raisins; set aside.
3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg — mix the baking soda in evenly to avoid green carrots.
4. In a large bowl, whisk together the applesauce, egg, honey, melted butter, and vanilla.
5. Stir half the flour mixture into the wet mixture, fold in the carrots and raisins, then stir in the remaining flour mixture.
6. Scoop about ¼ cup batter into each muffin cup.
7. Bake at 350°F for 22 to 27 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
8. Cool in the pan 2 minutes, then cool completely on a wire rack.
