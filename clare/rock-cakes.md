---
title: Rock Cakes
servings: 12 Rock Cakes
prep: 15 Min
cook: 17 Min
source: Adapted from Erin Jeanne McDowell's Drop Biscuits
---

## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
- ⅓ cup brown sugar
- 1½ tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 3 tsp ground cinnamon
- ½ tsp ground nutmeg
- ½ tsp ground allspice
- ¼ tsp ground cloves
- ¼ tsp ground ginger
- 6 tbsp butter, cold and sliced
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup milk
- ½ cup golden raisins
- ⅓ cup dried currants

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and spices.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg, vanilla, and ¼ cup of the milk.
4. Rub the butter into the flour mixture by hand until it reaches a breadcrumb consistency.
5. Add the egg mixture and stir with a spatula to combine.
6. Stir in the remaining milk a little at a time (up to ¼ cup at a time) until the dough reaches a soft dropping consistency — it drops off the spoon in about 5 seconds.
7. Fold in the raisins and currants in two or three batches.
8. Scoop into 12 rough balls on the baking sheet with an ice cream scoop; leave them craggy.
9. Bake at 400°F for 13 to 17 minutes, until golden on top and at the edges; the bottom should sound hollow when tapped. Cool on a wire rack.
