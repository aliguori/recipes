---
title: Mince Pies
servings: 12 Mince Pies
prep: 10 Min
cook: 18 Min
---

## Ingredients

- 1 package store-bought pie crusts
- 1 cup mincemeat
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
2. Unroll one pie crust. With a round cutter slightly larger than the pan's cups, cut 12 circles for the bottom crusts and gently press one into each cup.
3. Spoon mincemeat into each crust, no higher than level with the top of the crust.
4. Unroll the other crust. With a slightly smaller cutter (or decorative shapes), cut 12 top crusts and place them over the mincemeat.
5. Brush the tops with egg wash (1 egg beaten with 1 tbsp water).
6. Bake at 400°F for 13 to 18 minutes, until the tops are golden brown.
7. Cool slightly in the pan, then move to a wire rack to cool completely.
8. Optional: dust with powdered sugar.
