---
title: Wassail
servings: 16 Mugs
prep: 10 Min
cook: 10 Min
---

## Ingredients

- 2 quarts apple cider
- 5 cups pineapple juice
- 2 cups orange juice
- 1 cup lemon juice
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 1 tsp whole cloves

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients and bring to a simmer until the sugar is fully dissolved.
2. Transfer to a crockpot and keep warm for serving — it holds for several hours for a party.
3. Adults can add a splash of apple brandy (such as Laird's Apple Jack) to their mug.
4. Refrigerate leftovers; microwave a mug for 30 seconds to reheat.
