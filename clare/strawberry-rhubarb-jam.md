---
title: Strawberry Rhubarb Jam
servings: 2–3 Half-Pints
prep: 8 Hrs
cook: 10 Min
source: Adapted from The Complete Book of Small-Batch Preserving
---

## Ingredients

- 4 cups quartered strawberries
- 1 cup finely chopped rhubarb
- 2 cups sugar
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 1 vanilla bean pod, scraped

## Directions

1. Mix the strawberries, rhubarb, and sugar in a large bowl. Let stand 8 hours, stirring occasionally.
2. Transfer to a pot and bring to a boil over medium heat.
3. Add the lemon juice and vanilla bean, return to a boil, and boil rapidly 5 minutes.
4. Remove from heat, cover, and let stand 24 hours.
5. Bring to a full boil over high heat and boil rapidly 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
6. Skim off any foam and ladle into hot jars, leaving ¼″ headspace. Process in a boiling water bath 10 minutes.
