---
title: Rotini and Clare Sauce
servings: 2 Servings
prep: 5 Min
cook: 15 Min
---

## Ingredients

- 6 oz rotini
- 4 oz unsalted butter
- 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 oz finely shredded hard cheese
- ½ cup chopped parsley
- 2 tsp lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the rotini in salted water per the package instructions.
2. Melt the butter and mix it with the finely shredded hard cheese.
3. Drain the rotini, place in a bowl, and drizzle with olive oil to coat.
4. Mix the butter and cheese into the pasta.
5. Add the chopped parsley and lemon juice, then serve.
