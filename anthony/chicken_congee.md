---
title: Chicken Congee
servings: 2 Servings
prep: 10 Min
cook: Porridge Setting
---

## Ingredients

### Congee
- ½ rice cooker cup jasmine rice
- water, to the fill line
- 1 heaping tsp Better than Bouillon Roasted Chicken
- 1″ knob ginger, peeled
- 4 large garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 large chicken thigh
- salt

### To Serve
- 1 fried egg
- cubed fried Spam
- sliced green onions
- 1 tsp chili garlic oil

## Directions

1. Add the jasmine rice to the rice cooker and fill with water to the fill line.
2. Stir in the bouillon paste and soy sauce.
3. Microplane the ginger and garlic directly into the pot.
4. Debone the chicken thigh, season with salt, and slice against the grain. Add it to the pot, skin and all.
5. Cook on the porridge setting.
6. Serve topped with a fried egg, cubed fried Spam, sliced green onions, and chili garlic oil.
