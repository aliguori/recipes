---
title: Sunday Gravy
servings: 1 Large Pot
prep: 15 Min
cook: 1 Hr
---

## Ingredients

- 1 batch tomato gravy (see Tomato Gravy)
- 36 meatballs (see Meatballs)
- 2 packages Italian sausage (10 sausages)
- fresh basil

## Directions

1. After cooking the gravy, remove the pork ribs.
2. Cook the Italian sausage fully on the grill.
3. Cut the sausage into 1½″ slices and add to the gravy.
4. Add the meatballs to the gravy.
5. Coarsely chop the basil and add to the gravy.
6. Cook an additional 30 to 60 minutes.
7. Serve over pasta with bread and butter.
