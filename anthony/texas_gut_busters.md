---
title: Texas Gut Busters
servings: ~5 lb (2 kg Meat)
prep: 1 Hr + Overnight
cook: 5–6 Hr
---

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 1 kg beef chuck
- 1 kg pork shoulder
- 32mm natural hog casing

### Spice Mix
- 72.5 g BBQ Rub (see BBQ Rub)
- 60 g nonfat milk powder
- 5 g Cure #1
- 150 g cold water

## Directions

1. **Mix the spice mix:** combine the BBQ rub, milk powder, and Cure #1.
2. Cube the beef and pork to fit the grinder.
3. Grind all the meat once through a coarse plate.
4. Grind half the ground meat again through the coarse plate for varied texture.
5. Add the spice mix and cold water; mix thoroughly 5–10 min until extremely sticky.
6. Stuff into the hog casing.
7. Cure uncovered in the fridge overnight.
8. Smoke at 150°F for 2 hr, raise to 170°F for 1–2 hr, then 190°F until internal temp reaches 155°F.
9. Plunge into an ice bath to cool, then hang at room temperature to bloom at least 1 hr.
