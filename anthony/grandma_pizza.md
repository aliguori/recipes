---
title: Grandma Pizza
servings: 1 Large Pizza
prep: 20 Min
cook: 12–15 Min
source: Adapted from King Arthur Baking's Grandma Pizza
---

## Ingredients

### Crust
- 2 cups (212g) Italian-Style Flour
- 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour
- 1 tbsp pizza dough flavor (optional)
- 1¼ tsp (8g) table salt
- 2 tsp instant yeast
- 1¼ cups (283g) lukewarm water
- 2 tbsp (25g) olive oil

### For the pan
- 2 tbsp (25g) olive oil

### Topping
- 3 cups (340g) shredded mozzarella
- 1 can (12.3oz) Rao's pizza sauce
- pepperoni
- Locatelli pecorino romano, to finish

## Directions

1. **To make the crust:** Combine all crust ingredients in a large bowl; mix and knead by hand, mixer, or bread machine to form a smooth, elastic dough.
2. Place dough in a large bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate at least 4 hours or up to 24 hours.
3. **To assemble & bake:** Preheat the oven to 500°F.
4. Add 2 tbsp olive oil to a grandma-style pan or 18×13″ baking sheet, coating it. A dark pan gives the crispiest crust.
5. Turn the dough into the pan, gently stretching to the edges and corners. If it shrinks back, cover and rest 10 minutes, then resume.
6. Cover and let rest 30 minutes, until slightly puffed.
7. Top the crust with mozzarella all the way to the edges, dollop or spread the sauce over the cheese, then arrange pepperoni on top.
8. Bake in the bottom third of the oven 12 to 15 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden.
9. Finish with grated Locatelli pecorino romano.
