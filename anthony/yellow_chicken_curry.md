---
title: Yellow Chicken Curry
servings: 2 Servings
prep: 10 Min
cook: 25 Min
---

## Ingredients

- 1 can coconut milk (Chaokoh)
- 50g Mae Ploy curry paste
- 1 chicken breast
- 1 medium russet potato
- 1″ slice of a medium onion, quartered
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 dash fish sauce

## Directions

1. Heat a small pot over medium heat and add the oil.
2. Stir in the curry paste and sauté for a minute. If the paste absorbs all the oil, there isn't enough oil.
3. Turn down the heat and add ½ can of coconut milk.
4. Stir until it reaches a gentle simmer. From here on, don't over-boil the coconut milk or it will separate.
5. Cut the chicken breast into ½″ slices and add to the coconut milk.
6. Fill the coconut milk can with water, stir, and add to the pot.
7. Add the potato and onion and bring back to a simmer.
8. Let simmer 15 minutes.
9. Add the sugar and fish sauce, then serve over rice.
