---
title: Loaded Baked Potato
servings: 6 Halves
prep: 10 Min
cook: 1 Hr
---

## Ingredients

- 3 medium russet potatoes
- pulled pork
- BBQ sauce (Stubb's mesquite is good)
- shredded cheddar cheese
- sour cream
- 3 strips bacon, crumbled
- butter
- olive oil
- salt and pepper
- chives

## Directions

1. Cook the bacon ahead of time until crispy and break into bits.
2. Place the potatoes in a plastic bag and drizzle with oil, salt, and pepper, coating well.
3. Bake the potatoes at 350°F for 60 minutes.
4. With 25 minutes left, combine the pulled pork and BBQ sauce in a foil pouch and place in the oven to reheat.
5. Cut the potatoes in half lengthwise and place on a foil-lined baking sheet.
6. Lightly break up the potato insides and add a pad of butter to each.
7. Top with pulled pork, then sprinkle with shredded cheddar.
8. Broil until the cheese is melted.
9. Top each half with a dollop of sour cream, bacon bits, and chives, then serve.
