---
title: No-Fuss Focaccia
servings: One 9×13″ Loaf
prep: 7 Min
cook: 25–30 Min
source: Adapted from King Arthur Baking's No-Fuss Focaccia
---

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 5 tbsp (60g) olive oil, divided
- 1½ cups (340g) warm water
- 1¼ tsp (8g) table salt
- 3½ cups (420g) all-purpose flour
- 1 tbsp instant yeast
- 4 tsp pizza dough flavor (optional)
- ¼ cup (28g) cheddar cheese powder (optional)

### Topping
- 1 tbsp (13g) olive oil, for drizzling
- pizza seasoning (optional)

## Directions

1. Lightly grease a 9×13″ pan with non-stick spray, then drizzle about 2 tbsp of the olive oil over it.
2. Combine the remaining 3 tbsp olive oil with the rest of the dough ingredients and beat at high speed for 60 seconds.
3. Scoop the sticky batter into the prepared pan, cover, and let rise at room temperature for 60 minutes, until puffy.
4. While the dough rises, preheat the oven to 375°F.
5. Gently poke the dough all over with your index finger.
6. Drizzle lightly with olive oil and sprinkle with pizza seasoning and/or dried herbs as desired.
7. Bake until golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes.
8. Remove from the oven, wait 5 minutes, then turn out onto a rack. Serve warm or at room temperature.
