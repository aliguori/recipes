---
title: Carbonara
servings: 2 Servings
prep: 10 Min
cook: 15 Min
---

## Ingredients

- 6 oz spaghetti
- 2.5 oz diced pancetta
- 2 eggs
- 1 oz Locatelli pecorino romano
- pinch of salt
- pinch of cracked black pepper
- Italian parsley, to garnish

## Directions

1. Start boiling salted water for the spaghetti.
2. Sauté the pancetta over low heat.
3. Mix 1 egg and 1 egg yolk with the grated cheese, then season with salt and pepper and set aside.
4. Once the water boils, turn off the heat under the pancetta and let the pan cool.
5. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook per the package instructions.
6. When al dente, remove the pasta and add it directly to the pancetta.
7. Lightly toss the pasta and pancetta, then add the egg/cheese mixture.
8. Add a little pasta cooking water to thin the sauce if needed.
9. Serve with cracked pepper, grated cheese, and chopped parsley.
