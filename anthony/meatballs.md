---
title: Meatballs
servings: 32 Meatballs
prep: 15 Min
cook: 30 Min
---

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground chuck
- 1 lb ground veal
- 1 lb ground pork
- 1 medium onion, pureed
- 3 eggs
- ¾ cup grated romano cheese
- 4 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1½ tbsp dried basil
- 1½ tsp salt
- 1½ tsp pepper
- 1½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp crushed red pepper flakes

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
2. Combine all ingredients and mix well.
3. Divide into 32 meatballs.
4. Place on a greased half sheet pan.
5. Bake 30 minutes.
6. Let cool, then place under the broiler to crisp the outside.
