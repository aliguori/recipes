---
title: Boudin
servings: ~7 lb
prep: 30 Min + Overnight
cook: 4 Hr
---

## Ingredients

### Meat & Rice
- 2 kg pork shoulder
- 400 g chicken liver
- 2.4 kg cooked rice
- 32mm natural hog casing

### Aromatics
- 4 stalks celery, diced
- 2 medium onions, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 5 serrano peppers, seeded and diced
- 1 bunch green onions, sliced

### Seasoning
- 66 g salt + 6 g Cure #1
- 24 g granulated garlic, 6 g cayenne
- 12 g each white pepper, black pepper, paprika, and thyme

## Directions

1. Combine the pork, liver, and seasoning; refrigerate overnight.
2. Add the celery, onions, bell pepper, and serranos to a pot with the meat and enough water to cover.
3. Simmer 4 hr, then remove the meat and vegetables, reserving the liquid.
4. Crush the meat and vegetables, then mix in the cooked rice.
5. Stir in ~2 cups reserved liquid, taking as much fat as possible; add more until the consistency is right.
6. Stir in the green onions, then stuff into the casings.
