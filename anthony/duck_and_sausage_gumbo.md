---
title: Liguori Family Gumbo
servings: 1 Large Pot
prep: 30 Min
cook: ~4 Hrs
---

## Ingredients

- ½ lb smoked Andouille sausage (or any smoked sausage)
- 1½ lb duck meat (see Duck Stock & Fat)
- 1 qt duck stock
- 4 oz duck fat
- 4 oz flour
- 2 celery stalks
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 jalapeño
- 1 medium onion
- 4 cloves garlic
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tbsp filé powder
- ½ cup green onions, thinly sliced
- salt and pepper, to taste

## Directions

1. **Make the roux:** Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine the duck fat and flour in a heavy stock pot and bake 1½ hours, stirring every 30 minutes, to a brick-colored roux.
2. Slice the sausage into ½″ slices and chop the duck meat into ½″ chunks.
3. Dice the celery, bell pepper, onion, and jalapeño and add to the roux. Stir 7–10 minutes until the vegetables soften.
4. Add the garlic and stir 2 minutes more.
5. Add the bay leaves and duck stock and bring to a boil.
6. Add the duck meat and sausage and simmer 2½ hours.
7. Season with salt and pepper, stir in the filé powder, and take off the heat.
8. Add the green onions and serve over rice.
