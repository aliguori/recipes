---
title: Duck Stock & Fat
servings: ~3 Qt Stock
prep: 30 Min
cook: 2½+ Hrs
---

## Ingredients

- 1 whole fresh duck
- 2 stalks celery
- 2 carrots
- 1 medium onion
- 4 cloves garlic
- 2 bay leaves
- a dozen whole peppercorns
- 4 quarts water

## Directions

1. Remove the neck and organs from the cavity and place in a stock pot.
2. Remove the breasts and the thighs/legs; debone the thighs and legs. Reserve all this meat (~1½ lb) and refrigerate for the gumbo.
3. Carefully remove the skin and add it to the pot. Roughly break up the carcass and add it along with the leg/thigh bones.
4. Roughly chop the celery, carrots, and onion; smash the garlic. Add to the pot with the bay leaves, peppercorns, and water.
5. Simmer at least 2½ hours, skimming as needed.
6. Strain the stock into jars, removing the fat into a separate container. Chill and refrigerate.
7. Yields about 3 quarts stock, 5 oz duck fat, and ~1½ lb duck meat.
