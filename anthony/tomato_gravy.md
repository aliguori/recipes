---
title: Tomato Gravy
servings: 1 Large Pot
prep: 20 Min
cook: 3+ Hrs
---

## Ingredients

- 1 rack St. Louis-style pork ribs
- 3 cans crushed tomato
- 3 cans pureed tomato
- 2 cans water
- 2 small cans tomato paste
- 1 head garlic, minced
- 1 large onion, finely diced
- 1 tbsp oregano
- 1 tbsp thyme
- 1 tbsp dried basil
- 2 tbsp coarsely ground pepper
- 1 tbsp kosher salt
- ½ cup sugar
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Heat oil in a large stock pot until simmering.
2. Remove the membrane from the ribs, trim excess fat, and divide into 2-rib pieces.
3. Sear the ribs in small batches until browned on both sides, then set aside.
4. Cook the onion until slightly browned and translucent, then add the garlic and cook a couple minutes, careful not to burn.
5. Add the crushed and pureed tomatoes, water, and tomato paste.
6. Add the herbs, salt, and pepper along with the reserved ribs.
7. Simmer at least 3 hours, until the ribs are tender.
8. Remove and discard the ribs (or reserve for Sunday Gravy).
